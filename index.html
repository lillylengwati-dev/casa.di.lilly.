My Process

A simple, transparent workflow from first contact to launch

1

Discovery Call

We start with a friendly conversation on WhatsApp or phone. I'll ask about your business, goals, target audience, and what you want to achieve online.

What to Expect:

15-30 minute chat • No pressure • Free advice • Clear next steps

2

Proposal & Agreement

I'll send you a clear proposal outlining the scope, timeline, and pricing. No hidden fees or surprises—just straightforward terms.

What You'll Get:

Detailed scope • Fixed pricing • Realistic timeline • Payment terms

3

Content Gathering

I'll need some information from you: brand colors, logos, text content, photos, and any specific features you want. Don't worry—I'll guide you through what I need.

My Help:

Simple questionnaire • Content templates • Stock photo suggestions • Writing assistance if needed

4

Design & Development

I'll create your website or set up your social media/WhatsApp systems. This typically takes 1-3 weeks depending on the project complexity.

During This Phase:

Regular updates • Preview links • Open communication • Your input welcomed

5

Review & Revisions

You'll review everything and provide feedback. I include two rounds of revisions to make sure you're 100% happy with the final product.

What's Included:

2 revision rounds • Detailed feedback form • Video walkthroughs • Quick turnaround

6

Launch & Training

Once you approve everything, I'll launch your website or activate your systems. I'll also provide training so you can manage basic updates yourself.

You'll Receive:

Live website/system • Training session • Documentation • How-to videos

7

Ongoing Support

I don't disappear after launch. You'll get 30 days of free support for any questions or minor tweaks. After that, ongoing support packages are available.

Support Options:

30-day warranty • Technical assistance • Update services • Growth consultations
